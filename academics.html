Our Curriculum

Hifz-e-Quran

Rigorous memorization program of the Holy Quran, ensuring strict adherence to Tajweed rules and continuous revision strategies for students to preserve the Book of Allah in their hearts.

Nazra Quran

Reading the Holy Quran by looking at the text. We focus on teaching correct pronunciation (Makharij) and basics of Tajweed to build a robust foundation before moving on to Hifz or advanced studies.

Deeniyat

Fundamental teachings of Islam including Fiqh (jurisprudence), Aqaid (beliefs), Seerah (Life of the Prophet), and Akhlaq (character). Equips students with essential knowledge to lead a righteous daily life.

Arabic Studies

Comprehensive Arabic language curriculum. From basic grammar (Nahw and Sarf) to advanced literature, our goal is to enable students to directly comprehend the Quran and Hadith in their original language.

Urdu Studies

Building strong proficiency in Urdu reading, writing, and literature. Students explore classical texts and poetry to connect with historical Islamic scholarship and our rich cultural heritage.
