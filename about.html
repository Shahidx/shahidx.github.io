Our History & Values

Established with a profound dedication to Islamic education, Madarsa Abu Bakar Siddique has been a beacon of light in Hamirpur, Uttar Pradesh. Named after the first righteous Caliph of Islam, out institution embodies the virtues of truthfulness, steadfastness, and unwavering immense faith.

Our core values are deeply rooted in the teachings of the Quran and the Sunnah. We focus not only on rote memorization but on the comprehensive understanding of Deen (Religion). Modesty, respect for elders, kindness to all, and dedication to seeking beneficial knowledge are the pillars of our community.
